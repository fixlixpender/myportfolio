TOUCHLESS WEBSITE

MediaPipe Three.js JavaScript & HTML5

This is a futuristic, touchless website that you control entirely with your hand gestures in front of your webcam.

You don't need a mouse or keyboard. This application runs entirely in your web browser.


Vertical Scrolling: Raise your hand (open palm) to scroll the page Up. Lower your hand to scroll the page Down. The further you move your hand from the center, the faster it scrolls.


Page Navigation (Swiping): Move your hand to the Right edge of the screen to switch to the Contacts Page. Move your hand to the Left edge to swipe back to the Home Page.


Emergency Reset: Make a Fist (close your hand) at any time to immediately stop the engine and return to the main "Start" screen.


Visual Feedback: It shows a "digital mirror" of your hand with tracking dots so you know the computer sees you. The 3D background (floating shapes and stars) spins faster when you scroll, giving you a sense of speed.

Kinetic Core: AI-Driven Particle Interface

Three.js MediaPipe HTML5 & JavaScript WebRTC Canvas API

Kinetic Core is an immersive, browser-based interactive experiment that bridges the gap between physical human gesture and digital 3D art. By leveraging computer vision and WebGL, the application creates a "digital organism" composed of thousands of particles that react intelligently to the user's hand movements in real-time.


Unlike standard mouse interactions, this project offers a touchless interface. Users can "grab" the core by making a fist to trigger a dramatic, slow-motion particle explosion, or use two hands to physically stretch and expand the core's volume. It features a futuristic Heads-Up Display (HUD) that visualizes the AI's perception of the user's skeletal structure.
